Purpose

Everyone experiences stress, it's just that some of us process it differently and this can have a negative impact on our sense of wellbeing. If left unchecked, stress can get the best of us and we can be left feeling out of control. The Tense Less team are people just like yourself who experience stress daily but have come out with a range of products that are there to help you to be your best self.

Our Products

Our products are a tailor made range created to address daily factors that affect our perception of stress or directly impact our bodies neurochemical makeup.
